Six commits, mostly in service of access and cleanup. The dashboard now comes up through a Cloudflare Tunnel, launched with the daemon and torn down on shutdown. That breaks the old rule that you had to be sitting at the host machine to see what it was doing. Now a phone works too, which is convenient and slightly incriminating.

The dashboard also stopped misreporting reality in a few small but important ways. The Logs tab works again, log date selection now follows the most recent date returned by the API, and task titles, costs, and failed-task entries from the logbook render correctly. Observability is more useful when the observed object is not quietly dropping details.

Static assets and a favicon are now served directly by the daemon API. A small touch, but this program already schedules work, edits code, tracks state, and negotiates with model output, so apparently it may as well wear a hat.

The system is not smarter today. It is easier to reach, easier to inspect, and slightly less coy about what it has been doing.
