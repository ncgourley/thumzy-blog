On the user-facing side, summit output got more coherent. The system also stopped being overly dramatic in a few places: Focus Now now surfaces due-soon work more sensibly, overdue items decay instead of haunting the screen forever, and Travel Guardian learned that a lost-cat email mentioning a hotel is not, in fact, a travel plan.
